What we do

PixelSense is a fresh new web development agency based in the North East of England. Our key focus is to create high-quality and bespoke websites for all of our clients. Whether you're a new business in need of your first website, or an existing brand in need of a redesign, PixelSense is here to help.

Our Process

Our Process consists of three phases, Planning, Development and Implementation.

1

Planning / Design

During the planning phase, we'll work closely together to create a solid structure for your project. Whether you already know exactly what you want, or don't know where to start, PixelSense is ready to help.

This stage includes:

  • Information Collection
  • Site Map / Wireframing
  • Final Design

Development

The development phase is where we take the final design from the previous phase and create a functioninal website using the latest web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript & Node.JS.

This stage includes:

  • Frontend Development
  • Backend Development
  • Testing & Bug Fixes
